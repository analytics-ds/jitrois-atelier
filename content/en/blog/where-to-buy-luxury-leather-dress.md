Are you looking for the best place to buy a **high-end women's leather dress**? The choice of site matters as much as the choice of piece, because it determines the dress's authenticity, access to made-to-measure and the quality of guidance. Here are our markers for choosing the right address.

## In short

1. The best address for a **high-end leather dress** is the site of the house that designs it, for authenticity and made-to-measure.
2. Buying direct gives access to the full catalogue and a dedicated client service, which no marketplace truly replaces.
3. At Jitrois, each dress is made by hand in France in a stretch leather invented by the House, with the AGATHA dress at €2,690.
4. Made-to-measure is available even remotely, for a specific colour or size.

## Buying directly from the house, the first reflex

The best address for a luxury leather dress is the site of the house that creates it. Buying direct ensures a certain origin and access to the entire collection, not just a selection.

It is also the only way to benefit from the House's service and advice. At Jitrois, we designed jitrois.com to reproduce online the relationship you would have in store, from choosing the piece to made-to-measure.

On the site you find all of our [leather dresses](https://jitrois.com/collections/dresses), from the sculpted dress to the more pared-back piece, each made by hand in France.

## Stretch leather, a material found only here

The real distinguishing criterion of a high-end leather dress is the material. We invented **stretch leather** in the early 1990s, bonding the grain of plunged lambskin onto a cotton and Lycra base.

This leather follows the body like a second skin, without ever constraining it. It allows fitted cuts and constructions impossible with classic leather. We tell the story of this material on our [stretch leather page](https://jitrois.com/pages/materials).

It is this exclusivity that means a Jitrois leather dress is found only at Jitrois, a point to keep in mind when choosing where to buy.

## Made-to-measure, even online

Made-to-measure makes the difference on an exceptional piece. For a specific colour, size or length, our client service guides you, by email or phone, even remotely.

Few houses offer this service online. It turns a leather dress into a garment designed for you, something no multi-brand marketplace can offer.
